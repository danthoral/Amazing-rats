  • Rats can swim over a mile, some species can be in water for three days and hold their breath for three minutes.
  • Rats use their tail to keep them coool in the same way we humans sweat, they control their body temperature by expanding and contracting the blood vessels in their tails.
  • Rats can experience what scientists call "social - joy" while playing with humans and they can laugh, their laugh is a high- pitched noise.
